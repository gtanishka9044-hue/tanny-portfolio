## ğŸ¨ Quick Customization

### Change Your Name:
1. Open `index.html`
2. Find "Your Name" (Ctrl+F)
3. Replace with your actual name
4. Save (Ctrl+S)
5. Browser auto-refreshes!

### Change Colors:
1. Open `css/style.css`
2. Find `:root` at the top
3. Change `--primary-color: #6366f1;` to your color
4. Save and refresh

### Add Your Photo:
1. Place image in `images/` folder
2. Open `index.html`
3. Find `<i class="fas fa-user-circle"></i>`
4. Replace with: `<img src="images/your-photo.jpg" alt="Your Name">`
